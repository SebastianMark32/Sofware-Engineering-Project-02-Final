    New College of Florida Food Forest/Carbon Farm

    Students worked throughout the 2015-2016 school year to plant a Food Forest/Carbon Farm on the Caples Campus.It was officially planted on May 20, 2016, with over 50 different species of fruiting trees, edible greens, wildflowers, starch crops and spices. The Food Forest/Carbon Farm represents New Colleges commitment to finding innovative solutions to tough issues, as the Food Forest provides food for the campus, provides habitat for wildlife increasing local biodiversity and pollinators, facilitates study in a range of academic disciplines ranging from ecology to anthropology, all while sequestering excess carbon from the atmosphere to help mitigate climate change
